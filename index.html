Project 01 — Hackathon · KJ Somaiya IOM

Employee Attrition Prediction

Binary Classification · Ensemble ML · HR Analytics

End-to-end ML pipeline on 1,500 employees with a 10.5% minority-class imbalance. Engineered 6 domain features (Work Pressure Index, Commute Stress, Perf-Sat Gap), used 4-method feature selection, and trained Random Forest, XGBoost, and Gradient Boosting — choosing Recall as the business-critical metric because missing an at-risk employee costs more than a false alarm.
